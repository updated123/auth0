Thin JWT Architecture

Auth0 issues minimal tokens — the platform DB resolves all business context at runtime

Auth0 JWT contains ONLY:
sub · email · email_verified · exp
No roles, no permissions, no tenant data in the token
DB lookup on every request
Platform DB resolves:
org_id · role · entitlements · subscription · feature flags
Full business context resolved from sub claim
